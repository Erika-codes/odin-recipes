Authentic Beef Tacos

These amazing beef tacos are to die for! These tacos are prepared with beef, tomatos, onions, shredded cheese, and topped off with cilantro and a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Start off your Taco Tuesday right with these authentic, Mexican style tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 package of Taco seasoning
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 roma tomato, diced
  • 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup chopped Cilantro
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup cheddar Cheese, finely shredded
  • 12 Corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add beef. Cook and stir until browned, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add taco seasoning and follow the package instructions.
  4. Warm tortillas on a medium-high temperature griddle.
  5. Spoon beef over tortillas. Top with lettuce, Cheddar cheese, tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
